Stephen King

An Amazing Story Teller
Stephen King is an American author who writes horror, supernatural fiction, suspense, science fiction and fantasy books.

Information on Stephen King:

  1. King graduated from the University of Maine in 1970 with a bachelor's degree in English. To support himself writing stories, he worked odd jobs such as teaching or working as a janitor.
  2. His first published book was Carrie which appeared in 1974. The movies were created in 1976 and 2013 and they were successful.
  3. A lot of the books Stephen King wrote were so popular they were turned into tv-series or movies. His books such as The Shining, It, The Stand, and The Mist were incredibly successful movies.
  4. By the early 1990's King's name was synonymous with horror books and his books had sold more than 100 million copies by that time.
  5. King's family includes his wife, Tabitha King, and his two sons, Joe Hill and Owen King, all of whom are novelists.
